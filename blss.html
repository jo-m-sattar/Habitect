Bioregenerative Life Support

Closed-loop ecosystems for sustainable space habitation

Principle

Plants produce O₂ and food; humans provide CO₂ and waste. Properly managed, BLSS reduces resupply dependency and helps close mass & gas loops.

Components

Hydroponics/aeroponics growth racks, LED lighting, nutrient recovery & pumping systems, CO₂ scrubbing & distribution, water re-use (condensate + transpiration capture).

Design Notes

Plant species selection: leafy greens, dwarf tomatoes, fast-growing tubers; nutrient recipes adapted to hydroponic solution.

Lighting: LED spectra tuned to plant stage (blue/red ratio), PPFD target levels.

Monitoring: O₂, CO₂, humidity, root zone EC/pH, leaf disease monitoring (imaging).
