Sensor Systems

Category Sensor Type Purpose
Structural Integrity Strain gauges Measure stress/strain to detect overloads or deformation
Acoustic emission sensors Detect crack growth via ultrasonic emission
Ultrasonic transducers Find subsurface cracks/voids
Vibration sensors / accelerometers Detect abnormal vibration patterns (impacts, fatigue)
Fiber Bragg Grating (FBG) sensors Continuous embedded strain/temperature monitoring
Leak & Pressure Pressure sensors Detect cabin pressure drops (leaks)
Gas leak detectors Identify oxygen or contaminant leaks
Acoustic leak detectors (ultrasonic) Detect gas hissing
Environmental Temperature sensors (RTD/thermocouples) Monitor thermal stresses/overheating
Humidity sensors Prevent corrosion & moisture buildup
Radiation sensors Track cosmic radiation dose/rate
Imaging & Mapping High-resolution RGB cameras Visual inspection, AI crack detection
Infrared (thermal) cameras Detect leaks or stress via thermal anomalies
LiDAR / 3D scanners Map geometry & detect warping
Support & Navigation IMUs Stabilize navigation in microgravity
Proximity sensors (IR/ultrasonic/LiDAR) Collision avoidance & local mapping
Tactile/force sensors (robotic arms) Ensure correct contact pressure for repair
