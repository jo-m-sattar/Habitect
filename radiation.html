Radiation Monitoring

Protection from cosmic radiation threats

Threats

Galactic Cosmic Rays (GCR) and Solar Particle Events (SPE) cause ionizing radiation—dangerous for crew and electronics.

High dose events (>1000 mSv) can cause acute health issues.

Monitoring & Sensors

Continuous dose-rate monitoring (μSv/h), dose accumulation, and radiation event alarms.

Shielding approaches: material shielding (water, polyethylene, regolith), active shielding research (magnetic/plasma), adaptive shelter protocols.

Operational Response

Rigel should automate sheltering: when dose-rate exceeds threshold or predicted SPE, route crew to storm shelter and activate additional shielding strategies.
